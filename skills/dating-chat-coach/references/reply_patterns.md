# 回复模式

把下面的内容当作结构参考，不要机械照抄。要根据用户的语言习惯和具体聊天内容调整。

## 默认输出格式

```text
判断：对方正常接住了话题，也给了你一个可以延续的切口。先轻松回答，再反问。
建议发送：我今天也差不多，准备把节奏放慢一点。下雨天适合做点简单的饭，再看个不用太费脑子的东西。
备选：
- 更温和：听起来是个很舒服的宅家安排。我今天也准备待在家，可能做点简单的饭，再看部电影。
- 更活泼：这套室内避难方案很完整，我认可做饭加综艺的组合。
避免：不要只回复“你准备做什么饭？”，因为没有补充自己的信息。
下一步：如果对方补充了具体内容，就继续聊吃的或看的节目；如果对方再次反问，可以再增加一点自己的细节。
```

## 常见回复动作

回应对方并补充自己：

```text
That sounds like a calm weekend. I am also keeping it low-key, probably cooking something simple and watching a movie.
```

具体追问：

```text
What kind of shows are you in the mood for lately: comfort-watch, comedy, or something more serious?
```

轻松打趣：

```text
That is a very complete rainy-day plan. Food plus a show is hard to beat.
```

低压力邀约：

```text
If our schedules line up, we could grab coffee this weekend. Something easy, no elaborate plan needed.
```

修复：

```text
I just reread that and it came out more intense than I meant. I was mostly curious, not trying to interrogate you.
```

体面结束：

```text
Sounds like your week is packed. No pressure from me. We can pick this up when things settle down.
```

## 常见情境

对方回复很短：

- Send one reply that includes both a tiny self-disclosure and one easy hook.
- If short answers continue, lower investment.

对方问工作：

- Answer concretely.
- Add one human detail.
- Avoid status inflation.

对方问为什么使用交友软件或参加相亲：

- Be normal and grounded.
- Example: `I figured it is worth meeting people more intentionally. Apps are imperfect, but sometimes they do create real conversations.`

对方提到家庭：

- Mirror first.
- Avoid probing too fast.

对方提到前任：

- Stay mature and non-judgmental.
- Do not compete with or analyze the ex.

对方分享压力：

- Validate the specific situation.
- Ask what kind of support helps.

对方说最近很忙：

- Respect the schedule.
- Leave a low-pressure hook.

## 语气选项

最多提供三个：

- `steady`：平静、稳重，情绪强度较低。
- `playful`：更轻松、更会打趣，只在对方有回应时使用。
- `forward`：推进见面或澄清意图。
- `repair`：处理尴尬或表达过度。
